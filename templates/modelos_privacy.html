🔒 Privacy Policy

T-Lux Unlock Systems respects your privacy and is committed to protecting all information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when using our platform and services.

📘 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate our platform safely and efficiently, including:

  • Account information such as email address, name, and country.
  • Device identifiers (IMEI/Serial) used only for unlocking or verification purposes.
  • Payment and transaction details securely handled by Stripe or Flutterwave.
  • Technical logs required for platform diagnostics and anti-fraud measures.
🔐 2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Processing unlock services and API communications.
  • Account verification and license activation.
  • Security auditing, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.
🛡️ 3. Data Security

All communications between your device and T-Lux servers are encrypted with TLS 1.3. Sensitive information such as passwords and tokens is stored using secure, industry-standard encryption.

🌍 4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

T-Lux does not sell, share, or disclose personal information to third parties, except when required by law or for verified technical integrations (e.g., payment gateways or API suppliers).

⚙️ 5. Your Rights

You may request the correction or deletion of your data by contacting our support team via tluxunlock@t-lux.store. Account-related data required for financial or security compliance may be retained for audit purposes as permitted by law.

📅 6. Policy Updates

T-Lux may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect new legal or technical requirements. The latest version is always available on this page.

Contact: For privacy questions, email us at tluxunlock@t-lux.store.
